Day 2 :: Unconference

We have deliberately chosen to keep the agenda light and leave room for an Unconference. We believe that the best conversations happen in the hallways, and we want to make sure that there is plenty of time for that.

The second day will be decided on the moment and will take the form of an Open-Space. We did receive many applications for speakers, and we believe that a lot are also perfectly suitable for this format.

For more information, read about The Power Of Open-Space Technology.
